NP Membership is free for qualifying non-profits. Benefits include directory listing, event invitations, and volunteer notices.

Bronze members receive promotional listings, event discounts, and member newsletters.

Silver Membership — Benefits

Silver members receive featured placement, training sessions, spotlight opportunities, and larger discounts.

Gold members receive premium spotlight positions, sponsor mentions, VIP event access, and priority support.
